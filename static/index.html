Frequently Asked Questions

Is it safe to upload my PDF files to this tool?
Yes, your files are completely safe. All uploads are processed securely and deleted from our servers immediately after processing. We use encryption to protect your files during transfer and processing.
What file formats do you support?
We support PDF, Word (.docx), Excel (.xlsx), PowerPoint (.pptx), JPG, PNG, and HTML files for conversion. For PDF editing tools, we work exclusively with PDF files.
Is there a file size limit?
Yes, we support files up to 100MB per file. For optimal performance, we recommend keeping files under 50MB. Larger files may take longer to process.
Do you store or keep copies of my files?
No, we do not store or keep copies of your files. All uploaded files are automatically deleted from our servers after processing is complete, typically within minutes.
Can I merge password-protected PDFs?
Password-protected PDFs need to be unlocked before processing. You can use our "Unlock PDF" tool first, or provide the password during the upload process if that option is available for the specific tool.
How long does processing take?
Most operations complete within 10-30 seconds. Processing time depends on file size, number of pages, and the complexity of the operation. Large files or batch operations may take longer.
